# SIC Codes by Sector

## Overview

Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) codes are used to categorize companies by their primary business activity. This document maps the key sectors used in the ADR Stack Rank strategy to their corresponding SIC codes.

## 2. Energy Sector - ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Primary SIC Codes

#### **1310 - Crude Petroleum and Natural Gas**
- **Includes**: Oil and gas exploration, production
- **Examples**: XOM, CVX, COP, EOG, PXD
- **Sub-categories**:
  - 1311: Crude Petroleum and Natural Gas

#### **1380 - Oil and Gas Field Services**
- **Includes**: Drilling, well services, oilfield equipment
- **Examples**: SLB, HAL, BKR, NOV
- **Sub-categories**:
  - 1381: Drilling Oil and Gas Wells
  - 1382: Oil and Gas Field Exploration Services
  - 1389: Oil and Gas Field Services, Not Elsewhere Classified

#### **2910 - Petroleum Refining**
- **Includes**: Refineries, petroleum products
- **Examples**: MPC, VLO, PSX, PBF
- **Sub-categories**:
  - 2911: Petroleum Refining

#### **4610 - Pipelines, Except Natural Gas**
- **Includes**: Oil pipelines, product pipelines
- **Examples**: KMI, WMB, PAA
- **Sub-categories**:
  - 4612: Crude Petroleum Pipelines
  - 4613: Refined Petroleum Pipelines

#### **4920 - Natural Gas Transmission and Distribution**
- **Includes**: Natural gas pipelines, distribution
- **Examples**: EPD, ET, OKE, TRP
- **Sub-categories**:
  - 4922: Natural Gas Transmission
  - 4923: Natural Gas Transmission and Distribution
  - 4924: Natural Gas Distribution
  - 4925: Mixed, Manufactured, or Liquefied Petroleum Gas Production and/or Distribution

#### **4910 - Electric Services**
- **Includes**: Electric utilities, power generation
- **Examples**: NEE, DUK, SO, PPL
- **Sub-categories**:
  - 4911: Electric Services

#### **1200 - Coal Mining**
- **Includes**: Coal production (less relevant for modern energy)
- **Sub-categories**:
  - 1220: Bituminous Coal and Lignite Surface Mining
  - 1230: Bituminous Coal Underground Mining

### Key SIC Codes Summary for Energy:
- **1310**: Crude Petroleum and Natural Gas (Primary - Upstream)
- **1380**: Oil and Gas Field Services (Primary - Services)
- **2910**: Petroleum Refining (Primary - Downstream)
- **4610**: Oil Pipelines (Midstream)
- **4920**: Natural Gas Pipelines (Midstream)
- **4910**: Electric Utilities (Power Generation)

## Notes

- **SIC codes are 4-digit codes** (e.g., 7370)
- **Sub-categories** are typically 4-digit codes with specific meanings
- **Some companies** may have multiple SIC codes (primary and secondary)
- **NAICS codes** (North American Industry Classification System) are newer but SIC codes are still widely used
- **Your current system** uses sector names ("tech", "bio", "energy") - SIC codes would provide more precise filtering
