## 2026 Strategy Recommendations

### 1. **Optimize Filter Settings for 2026**

**Recommended Filters:**
```python
# Performance filters (moderate requirements)
min_1m_performance: 5-8%   # Recent momentum
min_3m_performance: 12-18% # Sustained trend
min_6m_performance: 20-30% # Longer-term strength

# ADR range (moderate volatility)
min_adr: 3%   # Ensure sufficient volatility
max_adr: 10%  # Avoid excessive whipsaw risk

# Price range (mid-cap focus)
min_price: $5
max_price: $150  # Broader range for opportunities
```

### 2. **Sector Allocation Strategy**

**2026 Sector Focus:**
- **40%**: Technology/AI (high momentum, high ADR)
- **20%**: Energy (rotation opportunities)
- **15%**: Healthcare/Biotech (event-driven)
- **15%**: Financials (rate cut beneficiaries)
- **10%**: Consumer Discretionary (recovery plays)

**Avoid:**
- Utilities, Consumer Staples (low ADR)
- Over-concentration in single sector

### 3. **Risk Management for 2026**

**Position Sizing:**
- **Base Case**: 3-4% per position (normal)
- **Bull Case**: 4-5% per position (increase)
- **Bear Case**: 1-2% per position (reduce)
- **Max Positions**: 12-15 stocks

**Stop Losses:**
- **Base Case**: 6-7% (standard)
- **Bull Case**: 7-8% (allow more room)
- **Bear Case**: 4-5% (tighter)
- **Trailing Stops**: 5% from peak (after 10% gain)

**Take Profits:**
- **10% gain**: Take 30% position
- **15% gain**: Take another 40% position
- **20%+ gain**: Take remaining 30%
- **40-day max**: Exit regardless if no progress

### 4. **Entry Timing for 2026**

**Best Entry Conditions:**
- ✅ 3-5 days of consistent momentum
- ✅ Volume increasing (1.5x average)
- ✅ RSI 45-65 (not overbought, not oversold)
- ✅ Breaking above recent resistance
- ✅ Sector showing strength
- ✅ Market in risk-on mode

**Avoid Entry:**
- ❌ After 7+ days of strong moves (exhaustion)
- ❌ RSI > 75 (overbought)
- ❌ Low volume moves (weak participation)
- ❌ During market-wide selloffs
- ❌ When sector is weakening

### 5. **Exit Strategy for 2026**

**Profit-Taking:**
- **Quick Wins**: 8-10% in 5-10 days → Take 50%
- **Normal Wins**: 12-15% in 15-25 days → Take 75%
- **Extended Wins**: 20%+ in 30+ days → Full exit

**Stop Losses:**
- **Hard Stop**: 7% loss (no exceptions)
- **Trailing Stop**: 5% from peak (after 10% gain)
- **Time Stop**: Exit if no progress after 25 days

**Momentum Fade Exits:**
- Volume declining for 3+ days
- RSI dropping below 40
- Price breaking below 20-day moving average
- Sector showing weakness
