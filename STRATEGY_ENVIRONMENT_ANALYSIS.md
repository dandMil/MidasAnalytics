# ADR Stack Rank Strategy: Economic Environment Analysis

## Strategy Characteristics

**Core Components:**
- **High ADR Focus**: Ranks stocks by Average Daily Range (volatility)
- **Performance Filters**: Requires positive 1M/3M/6M performance (momentum)
- **Short-Term Orientation**: Designed for active trading (40-day max hold)
- **Momentum-Based**: Captures stocks with recent upward price movement

## Recommendations for Current Environment

### 1. **Adapt Filter Settings**

**For Current Market:**


### 2. **Sector Focus**

### 3. **Risk Management Adjustments**

**For 40-Day Holds:**
- **Stop Loss**: 5-7% (tighter than longer holds)
- **Take Profit**: 10-15% (realistic for short-term)
- **Max Hold**: 40 days (as specified)
- **Position Size**: 2-3% per position (diversification)

### 4. **Entry Timing**

**Best Entry Conditions:**
- ✅ Stock showing 3+ days of momentum
- ✅ Volume increasing
- ✅ RSI 40-60 (not overbought yet)
- ✅ Breaking above recent resistance
- ✅ Sector showing strength

**Avoid Entry:**
- ❌ After 5+ days of strong moves (exhaustion risk)
- ❌ RSI > 70 (overbought)
- ❌ Low volume moves
- ❌ During market-wide selloffs

### 5. **Exit Strategy**

**Take Profits:**
- 10% gain: Take 50% position
- 15% gain: Take remaining 50%
- 20%+ gain: Full exit

**Stop Losses:**
- Hard stop: 7% loss
- Trailing stop: 5% from peak (after 10% gain)

**Time-Based Exits:**
- If no progress after 20 days: Consider exit
- If momentum clearly faded: Exit early
- Don't hold to 40 days "just because"
